More About Me

What am I up to?

Hive Helsinki Studies

In Fall 2019 I started my journey to become a software engineer. My main focus at the moment is learning how to code at Hive Helsinki, a new coding school at which I was one of the first-ever students. All of my projects for the curriculum can be found on my GitHub.

Honey the Hackathon

Honey is our so-called passion project. Together with some fellow Hive students we put together a hackathon concept to connect Hive students with industry professionals. You can read more about it here.

Hexagon ry

I am one of the founding members and member of the board at Hexagon ry, the student organization at Hive Helsinki. I am responsible for external relations and so far I have, apart from the actual founding of the organization, organized events, negotiated partnerships, and organized excursions.

Aalto University Studies

I did my B.Sc. degree in International Business and currently doing my M.Sc. in Information and Service Management focusing on Information Systems Science.
